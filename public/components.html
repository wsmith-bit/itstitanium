FAQ example

Is titanium cookware the same as titanium-reinforced nonstick?
Not exactly. Pure titanium pans are uncommon outside backpacking gear. Many consumer “titanium” pans are aluminum with a titanium-reinforced nonstick coating.
Can I use metal utensils?
Use silicone or wood tools to protect coatings.
Is it induction compatible?
Only if the base includes magnetic steel.
