FAQ

Which titanium cookware brand has the best warranty?
All-Clad offers lifetime coverage on the base with multi year coating support when you follow care instructions, while most value brands offer two year plans.
Do budget titanium pans match premium heat performance?
Budget pans like T-fal Ultimate stay within 18°F variance, yet premium lines sit closer to 12°F, so searing is more predictable with higher end sets.
Which brand is best for induction cooktops?
All-Clad FusionTec and Scanpan HaptIQ have full magnetic bases. Ninja models vary by SKU, so confirm packaging details.
