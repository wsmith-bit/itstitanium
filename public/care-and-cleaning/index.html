Daily cleaning routine in four steps

  1. Cool the pan for two minutes. Hot rinses can warp the aluminum core.
  2. Wash with a soft sponge and mild soap. Avoid powders unless you are doing a quarterly refresh.
  3. Rinse with warm water. Wipe rivets with a brush to remove trapped oil.
  4. Dry completely. Use a microfiber towel, then hang or add a felt protector if stacking.

Care FAQ

Can I use metal utensils?
We recommend silicone or wood utensils. Brands like Scanpan advertise metal safe coatings, but gentle tools extend release quality by months.
What oils are safe for high heat?
Choose avocado, refined peanut, or grapeseed oil. They have smoke points above 400 Fahrenheit and match Serious Eats oil testing.
How do I store titanium pans?
Hang them or use pan protectors between stacks. Avoid stacking without padding, which causes micro scratches that reduce release.
